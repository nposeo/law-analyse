Using GPT-4o with adversarial review, we parse PDF laws, extract key information, and provide plain language explanations. Built with FastAPI, Astro, and React, it's production-ready, open source, and costs $0 to host. Perfect for legal professionals, students, and citizens who need to understand Ukrainian laws."

- **Smart Parsing:** Extract articles with hierarchy preservation

- **Plain Language:** Toggle between legal and simplified text
- **Confidence Scoring:** Auto-flag uncertain extractions for human review

- **Open Source:** MIT license, contribute on GitHub
